FAQ

If I do not meet the recommended hours, should I even bother applying to the college?

These hours are just recommended. However, by obliging to the hours, you not increase the chances of going to the college, but also gain a massive assortment of skills, such as listening, working as a team, and leadership.

Why are volunteer hours important?

The importance is volunteer hours , like mentioned before, will not only increase your chances of getting into the dream college, but will also build a massive assortment of skills, such as listening, working as a team, and leadership.

How can I, as a parent, help my daughter/son in their volunteer hours?

As a parent, your role is the most important. It is even more important than that of your child's. It is your job to provide support for your child, no matter the place, career, major, or job they choose. Many are already demonstrating this perfectly. Therefore, the main thing you must do is to provide support.

Are these numbers exact?

Every single number is taken from the official websites of these respective schools. Some numbers are taken from trusted students and alumni.

How do I find a place to volunteer at?

Normally, you will want to look for want ads that you can find by walking down the street. Another good way to find volunteering places is through your school counselor. They have the ability to refer you to previous places where others have volunteered.
