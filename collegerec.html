Extremely Selective Colleges:

College selectivity is a measure of how difficult it is for students to get admitted. A Selective College Is Simply a College That Does Not Admit Everyone Selectivity is measured by the percentage of students who are admitted. The lower the percentage, the more selective the school is. Extremely selective schools only admit 15% or less of their applicants. These schools include
